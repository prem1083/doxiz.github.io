Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At Doxiz, our mission is to empower firms and legal teams to draft documents smarter, faster, and more accurately. We simplify legal workflows with technology that boosts efficiency, reduces manual effort, and ensures consistency—so firms can focus on delivering greater value to their clients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to redefine the future of drafting through innovation and simplicity. We aim for a world where professionals collaborate seamlessly, manage documents with confidence, and leverage intelligent tools that keep them ahead in a digital-first legal industry.

About Doxiz

Doxiz was created to modernize how firms and legal teams handle document drafting. Built on Microsoft technologies, it brings powerful automation without disrupting your current workflow. With a smart clauses and documents library, lawyers can prepare documents faster while maintaining consistency and accuracy.


The seamless Word Add-in ensures that teams work within the tools they already use every day. Doxiz also supports multi-tenant SaaS, making it scalable across multiple firms and departments. Upcoming integrations with iManage and NetDocuments further enhance document management efficiency. Security and compliance are at the core, with features like audit logs, role-based access, and secure storage. In short, Doxiz helps professionals save time, reduce errors, and deliver higher value to clients.
